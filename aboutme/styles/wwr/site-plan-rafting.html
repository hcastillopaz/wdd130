Overview

Purpose

Our roots lie deep in the Chamelecon River. Raftotality is the first oar powered outfitters to run on the upper Chamelecon River. We have built our operations to be based upon delivering superior personal service, hiring only the best guides and using the highest quality, highly maintained equipment to share the Chamelecon River with our guests. We are proud to offer Chamelecon river rafting trips, guided fishing and cabin rentals all on the beautiful Upper Chamelecon River and thru the Merendon National Wildlife Refuge thru our special use permits.As one of the newest and locally owned rafting companies on the upper Chamelecon River, Raftotaly Company is uniquely positioned to provide you a spectacular vacation or quick getaway that you will treasure forever.
